Our Manifesto

Written by the iDENTify Team

Our goal is simple: Reforming the dental industry.

We are disgusted. Whether it's the lack of early detection tools, the anxiety of not knowing if a symptom is serious, or the late-night panic when pain strikes—or the constant stream of emergency texts disrupting schedules, the inability to plan effectively, or the burnout from reactive care—we find the status quo a disservice to both patients and dental professionals.

But we strongly believe technology is still a beautiful solution. This is why:

For Patients

  • Late-night emergencies are preventable. Early detection changes everything.
  • Patients deserve peace of mind from instant guidance.
  • Accessible monitoring is a reliable health source.

For Dentists

  • Emergency appointments destroy clinic efficiency. This is unsustainable.
  • Dentists deserve predictable schedules and reduced emergency visits.
  • Early detection is a reliable workflow stabilizer.

Our realer goal: Build our way to becoming the catalyzing force of the preventive dental care era.
