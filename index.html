The Emergency Appointment Crisis

For Patients

After surgery, your only guidance is texting a busy dentist. Most patients ignore early warning signs until it's too late—resulting in painful emergencies and expensive treatments.

For Dentists

Individually messaging thousands of patients is impossible. Emergency appointments disrupt your meticulous schedule, forcing you to squeeze in urgent cases while other patients wait.

The Late-Night Reality

Patients frantically text at 2 AM about sudden toothaches. Dentists can't respond to everyone. The system is broken for both sides.

Our Solution: Peace of Mind in Your Pocket

iDENTify is a smartphone app that works with a 3D-printed attachment to let patients take clear intra-oral scans at home. Our machine learning model analyzes these scans to detect early signs of caries, gingivitis, and periodontitis—catching problems before they become emergencies.

For Patients

Get accessible feedback anytime. Catch issues early. Stop worrying about whether that tooth pain is serious.

For Dentists

Reduce emergency appointments. Monitor patients remotely. Focus on scheduled care instead of crisis management.

The Result

Fewer emergency appointments. Less stress for everyone. Better oral health outcomes.

Our AI model is trained on extensive dental imaging data to provide reliable early detection. Validated with 95% accuracy in beta testing.
